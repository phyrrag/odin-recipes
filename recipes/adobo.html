Chicken Adobo

Iconic Dish of the Philippines!

Chicken Adobo is one of the most popular dishes in the country! It is sweet with a hint of saltiness from the soy sauce. It is then added with bay leaves and pepper corns for a more flavorful taste. Perfect for any occassions.

Ingredients

  • 1 whole Chicken
  • 1 Onion
  • 1 Garlic
  • 1/4 cup Soy Sauce
  • 1/4 cup Vinegar
  • 2 tsp. Cooking oil
  • 1/4 cup Water
  • 1/4 cup Brown Sugar
  • Bay leaves
  • Peppercorns

Steps

  1. Butcher the chicken into pieces
  2. Marinate the chicken with soy sauce and garlic. Let it marinate for at least 30 minutes

    3. Note: Do not throw the marinade! Keep it aside

  3. After marinating, heat up the oil on a cooking pan
  4. Stir fry the chicken until golden brown
  5. Seperate the chicken and saute the onions
  6. Add the stir fried chickens and the marinade
  7. Add the vinegar (Do not stir for a couple of seconds)
  8. Add sugar, bay leaves, and peppercorns
  9. Add water and let it boil for at least 30 minutes.
  10. Serve it hot and enjoy!
