Tortang Talong

Quick and Easy dishes!

Tortang Talong is one of the quickest and easiest dishes to make. It is also very healthy and only needs 2 ingredients. Perfect for a healthy and budget consious eaters! We're going to make 2 delicious tortas!

Ingredients

  • 2 Eggplants
  • 2 Eggs
  • Cooking oil
  • Salt and Pepper

Steps

  1. Roast the eggplants until the skin starts to peel off (Alternatively, You can also boil the eggplants until it's fully cooked)
  2. Set aside the roasted or boiled eggplants to let it cool
  3. Beat the eggs and season it with salt and pepper
  4. Peel the skin off the eggplants
  5. Put the eggplants on the egg and start pressing it with a fork until it flattens. Make sure that the eggplant is well coated (Don't press it too much)
  6. Heat up the oil on a pan
  7. Fry the eggplants till golden brown
  8. Once cooked, drain the excess oil
  9. Serve and enjoy!
